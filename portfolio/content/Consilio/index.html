Consilio

Projects made manageable.

Goal

Most students have to cope with three things in general: school, work and friends and family. These are all lovely things to spend time on or with, but most of the time we have to choose two of the three because of a lack of time. This causes frustration, loneliness or dissapointing school results.

Consilio solves this time management problem. The service allows you to create and manage you tasks. After you have created a project it devides the tasks evenly over the teammembers depending on duration and plans in their existing agenda. It is posible to change the planned events after this process.

Research

Needs

  • Enough time for school, work, family and friends
  • A project management solution for all our shared projects
  • Sharing data and files of the projects

Key Functions

  • A personal agenda with all task and events
  • The Creation, Edit and Delete functionality for projects and tasks
  • A dashboard which give you direct information about individual projects
