Voiage

Planned relaxation during public transport usage.

Goal

We all consume media content during our travels between school, work and home. Many of us get frustrated with this habit. This is never fitting playist. Wether it is to short or to long we always suffer from a dead end.

Voiage resolves this problem by using data from your YouTube account and your travel plan. As soon as you have choosen your route the app will calculate a perfect fitting playlist based on the duration of your travel.

Research

Needs

  • Relaxation after work, school or other tiresome things
  • A good fitting playlist so we don't have to worry about durations

Key Functions

  • Planning a travel route between two points, your current location is your start point
  • Calculation of the best fitting playlist based on your travel duration and personal playlists
  • A surprise button to completely get of road
