An immersive storytelling game about the city of tomorrow.

Future of the City

In what way will our cities change in the coming 30 years? Would they be cleaner, more sustainable and livable? Or will they be plageued by new diseases, be more stressfull to live in and more poluted then ever? The outcome would probably be something in the middle of these scenarios. And each outcome will have it's pro's and con's.

One of the most best ways to explore an scenario is to take part of that scenario. Creating a game is probably one of the most immersive ways. Therefore we created a game which speculates on the future of Bogota, Colombia.

Speculating on the future

Extrapolating current developments in Bogota.

  • 2020

    Because of the growing population the Begotan City councils decides in favor of the compact city model. This means efficient traffic and the focus high-rise buildings.

  • 2025

    The slums cause problems during the development of the new city model. Therefore it is decided to enforce investments and regulation. Govermental organizations start with buying giant pieces of land and assign these to the project which fullfill the requirements of the new city model. The slums will be replaced by cheap homes affordable for the old slum dwellers through govermental grants.

    Besides enforcing the city development the city council decides the invest in public transport and cultural projects.

  • 2035

    Several city restructering projects have been extremely succesfull. The biggest part of the slums are replaced by new, modern and affordable homes. Tourism is triving due to the cleansed image of the city and the investment in cultural projects.

    The goverment decides to start social projects to create a new future for old FARC members. These members can rehabilitate And join serveral city development organizations and projects.

  • 2040

    The left-wing rehabilitated old FARC members are causing a rise corruption and white-collar crime. The goverment tries to hide this by controlling streetart and spreading propaganda.

    Due to the rising corruption money for the building and social projects is vanishing.

  • 2050

    The development of the new city is in danger due to the lack of the money. The pride of the city is at stake and therefore the city council tries to control the cultural projects of the city in an rigorous way.

Story Setup

Ella's father has been vanished when she was still a young girl. Now that's she is growing up she is seeking for identity. She is missing her father in finding herself in a rapidly chaning world. She starts doing research on the back story of her father. The more she is finding out about her father the more filthy stories she discovers. She get's involved in antigovermental gangs who's through changing the propaganda streetart during the night seek to uncover the corruption inside the coverment.

Gameplay

The prototype of the game concists of the first level of the game. In this level the player explores the key gamemechanics as well as the new south of the city of Bogota. This part of the city was previously a slum. Nowadays it's a pleasant area full of small houses near the city center. The key gamemechanics are the following:

  • Walking using the A, S, W, D keys or the arrowkeys
  • Interacting with objects using the E key
  • Taking photo's using the photo interface
