Simple CMS

Website and Data Management simplyfied.

Goal

Many website owners do want to update their customors about their products, services or special events. The problem is that most website maintainers aren't coders or webdesigners. Most of the time they're day to day marketing or pr guys who do now the web but don't fully master the underlying technology.

This Content Management system solves this problem by making it posible to maintain a website without the use of code and without the irritation of an extremely clustered interface.

Research

Needs

  • Content Management without any technical knowledge
  • A website specific website builder
  • Sharing and Management of several datatypes

Key Functions

  • A fast codeless pagebuilder build with a modular framework
  • Management of emails, files, accounts and other data using a modular framework
  • A dashboard which give you direct information about your website
